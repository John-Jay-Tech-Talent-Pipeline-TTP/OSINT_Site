    <h1>Google Dorking</h1>
    <p>What is Google Dorking?</p>
    <p>It is basically a way hackers use to get through a site to find if they have any security holes in the computer codes in order to get the sites valuable and non-public information.</p>
    <ul> Examples where Google Dorking is used:
      <li>Ethical Hacking (Kali Linux, Kali Nethunter) </li>
      <li>Data Collection Phase of OSINT</li>
    </ul>

    <ul> Pros
      <li>Efficient way of utilizing Google Search Engine</li>
      <li>Can recover an abundance of information that may not be that visible on a website</li>
    </ul>

    <ul> Cons
      <li>Can be illegal if used for malicious purposes (e.g. cyberattack reconnaissance)</li>
      <li>Can collect sensitive info from results</li>
      <li>Could be used to exploit security vulnerabilities of a website</li>
    </ul>
